# Privacy Policy for PocketWallet

**Last Updated: June 14, 2026**

Your privacy is important to us. This Privacy Policy explains how PocketWallet ("we", "our", or "us") collects, uses, and protects your information when you use our mobile application.

## 1. Information We Collect
To provide you with the best experience, we collect the following limited data:
* **Account Information:** When you sign up, we collect your email address and password securely via Supabase authentication to manage your account access.
* **Financial Data:** All income, expense, and budget tracking data you enter into the app is securely stored in our database solely to display your personal financial analytics.

## 2. How We Use Your Information
We use the collected data strictly for the following purposes:
* To create and maintain your personal PocketWallet account.
* To process and synchronize your income and expense data across your devices.
* To provide you with your automated spending charts and financial analysis.

## 3. Data Protection and Third Parties
* **No Data Sharing:** We do not sell, trade, or share your personal or financial data with third-party companies, advertisers, or any outside entities.
* **Security:** Your data is encrypted and managed using industry-standard secure infrastructure.

## 4. Your Rights and Data Deletion
You retain complete ownership of your financial data. You have the right at any time to request the absolute deletion of your account and all associated transactional data from our systems.

## 5. Contact Us
If you have any questions about this Privacy Policy or wish to request data deletion, you can contact us directly through our official GitHub repository page or support channels.
